    Final Year Project (Bachelor's)

    Augmented CPR+

    Details

    As the team lead of Augmented CPR+, We developed next-generation CPR training system designed to revolutionize emergency preparedness. Built on fusion of Augmented Reality and CPR Training, the system provides real-time feedback on compression rate and depth, guiding users through every critical step of high-quality CPR. Visual AR cues assist with rhythm, hand placement, and airway management, while an interactive simulation of the RESQCPR (composed of both RESQPUMP and RESQPOD) enables safe, advanced CPR training. Our device features offline capability through MQTT communication with upto 8 devices, allowing it to run seamlessly without internet. Versatile in design, it can operate standalone, with a mobile app, or through AR glasses. The compact, ergonomic hardware includes a custom 3D-printed casing and magnetically attachable profiles for different chest anatomies. Augmented CPR+ was awarded 1st Prize in the HCD 3.0 Competition by the MADE Foundation, received a Special Prize at DUHS-DICE innovative Exhibition, secured the NGIRI Ignite Scholarship, and proudly represented UET Lahore at Pintech Expo 2025.
